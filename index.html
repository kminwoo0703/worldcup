Event Overview

The world's biggest event in 2022 happened in Qatar. FIFA World Cup had the greatest ending in World Cup history. This match was especially interesting because they faced each other in last World Cup in 2018. In that year, France won the World Cup. For Argentina, this was a revenge match of 2018.

Messi wins his first World Cup title

Lionel Messi finally won the world cup title. This was the only accomplishment that Messi did not have in his career. This title made him undeniable GOAT as his rival, Cristiano Ronaldo, does not have a World Cup title.

Mbappe the new Super Star?

Kylian Mbappe scored All 3 goals for France to tie the game. Argentina was up 2-0 up until minute 80 where Mbappe scored 2 goals in 2 minutes. The game went to overtime and Lionel Messi scored the go-ahead goal. Soon after, Mbappe scored a goal to tie the game.
Everyone has been guessing who will be the next super star in soccer after Messi and Ronaldo. Fans are expecting Mbappe to be one of the superstar for the next generation.
